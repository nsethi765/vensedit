  1. Nov, 1999
    Foundation of PLCIE

    PLCIE was founded by Mr. B.P Arun with an aim to promote the “Robotics & Embedded Systems” to provide automation to reduce the manpower from industry in today’s scenario where every work is to be done automatically. PLCIE was set up to provide the best learning in the field of “Robotics & Embedded Systems” to the students who are studying engineering in various disciplines and to all those who want to make their career in the field of “Robotics & Embedded Systems” for Automation industry.

  2. Sept, 2003
    Foundation of Robovatika

    Robovatika is an Electronics, Automation & Industrial Components (CNC Machine) Trading Company Founded in 2003. Robovatika also Imoprt & Export mechanical equipments specifically related to Automation Systems, Micro Controller systems, PCBs, etc.

  3. 2017
    Foundation of Tayc Inc.

    Tayc Inc. was founded by LP Arun to guide his trading business and technology-focuses ventures. LP Arun then founded Vensed Enterprises "Which was formerly named Vensed in 2017, A electronics trading company" and later PLCIE and Robovatika joined with the aim to from the world's client's company.
